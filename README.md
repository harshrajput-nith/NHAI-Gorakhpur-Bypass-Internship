# NHAI Highway Construction Internship Report

This repository contains my internship report completed during my training at the **National Highways Authority of India (NHAI), PIU Gorakhpur**.

The internship provided practical exposure to highway construction, quality control testing, bridge construction, reinforced earth structures, and project management practices followed in large-scale infrastructure projects.

## Project Overview

The project involved the construction of the **4-Lane Greenfield Northern-East Side Gorakhpur Bypass**.

- **Length:** 26.616 km  
- **Start Point:** NH-29E (Chainage 83.308)  
- **End Point:** NH-28 (Chainage 285.100)  
- **Contract Mode:** EPC (Engineering, Procurement and Construction)  
- **Contractor:** Vijai Construction (India) Pvt. Ltd.

The objective of the bypass is to reduce congestion inside Gorakhpur city and improve regional connectivity.

## Key Learning Areas

### Highway Engineering
- Pavement structure (Flexible & Rigid pavement)
- Bituminous layers such as DBM and BC
- Wet Mix Macadam (WMM) and Granular Sub-Base (GSB)

### Quality Control Tests
Laboratory and field testing for highway materials including:

- Soil Tests (CBR, Atterberg Limits, Free Swell Index)
- Aggregate Tests (Impact Value, Water Absorption)
- Bitumen Tests (Penetration, Ductility, Softening Point)
- Concrete Tests (Slump Test, Cube Compressive Strength)

### Bridge Engineering
- Major bridge construction
- Bored cast-in-situ pile foundations
- PSC girder superstructure
- Post-tensioning systems

### Reinforced Earth (RE) Wall
- RE panel erection
- Geostrap reinforcement
- Drainage layer and geocomposite systems
- Backfill compaction and alignment control

### Surveying
- Use of Total Station for alignment and layout
- Structural drawing interpretation

### Contractual Framework
- EPC contract structure
- Contractor responsibilities
- Defect Liability Period (DLP)

### Construction Safety
- Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)
- Traffic control measures
- Heavy machinery safety procedures

## Key Skills Gained

- Understanding of real highway construction practices
- Quality control and testing procedures
- Structural and pavement engineering concepts
- Surveying and site layout techniques
- Exposure to large infrastructure project execution
